About PhishGuard

PhishGuard is an advanced AI-powered tool designed to detect phishing attempts in emails. Our system uses machine learning algorithms to analyze various aspects of an email, including its content, links, and linguistic patterns to determine if it's a legitimate message or a phishing attempt.

Phishing attacks are becoming increasingly sophisticated, making them harder to detect with traditional methods. PhishGuard leverages artificial intelligence to stay ahead of these evolving threats.

How It Works

PhishGuard analyzes emails using a multi-layered approach:

  1. Text Analysis: We examine the language and content of the email for suspicious patterns.
  2. URL Detection: Our system checks for suspicious links, including shortened URLs and typosquatted domains.
  3. Linguistic Analysis: We identify urgent language and requests for sensitive information.
  4. Machine Learning: All these features are combined using advanced machine learning to provide an accurate assessment.

Simply paste the email subject and body into the form above, and our AI will analyze it instantly, providing you with a detailed risk assessment.
